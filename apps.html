Service
Why I Use It
Gmail
My primary inbox. I use Cloudflare Email Routing to forward my domain email here, allowing me to use my old Gmail address which integrates seamlessly with other services.I tried to avoid using Gmail for years, but the integration with other services is too good to avoid. Would attach domain to Workspace, but I lose some stuff I like in the consumer grade Gmail.
Todoist
My central task manager. It replaced Apple Reminders. The integration with Gmail is great, and the natural language input is so so good!
Spotify
I have gone back and forth between Spotify and Apple Music. I like Apple Music's look and the fact it does not force Podcasts in the app, however Spotify does a much better job with Playlists so here I am.
Apple Notes
Seamless integration across my devices for quick thoughts and simple organization.
Google Drive & Docs
Just works for what I need.
Google Gemini
I don't use AI to write any of my own words, but it is pretty good when I am playing with code like for this site.
Perplexity
Really is a great replacement search engine clearly showing sources for all answers.
Raindrop.io
All-in-one bookmark manager for saving articles and links.
Textastic
A versatile code editor for iPad and Mac.
Working Copy
Best way to manage Git on iOS
Apple Maps
Google Maps got too cluttered, so Apple Maps it is.
Overcast
My favroite podcast app, though others like Pocketcasts are fine too.
Reeder (iOS)
The best RSS reader for a clean reading experience.
Instapaper
Post Pocket leaving the world I went back to Instapaper and am very happy.
NYT Games
Everyday I play Wordle and complete both crosswords, mini and regular
Duolingo
One lesson a day keeps the brain going.
