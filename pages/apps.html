Apps/Services I use

Phone: iPhone 13 Mini I love this form factor and have passed on upgrades the last few cycles since they got rid of the small phone. This year I will have to find a new device, and for the first time in a decade I might sway away from iOS.
E-Mail Service: Gmail Pretty self explanatory.
E-Mail App: Gmail I have tried many third party apps and used the iOS built in mail app for yeras, but when it comes to using gmail features like labels, using the official Gmail app just works better.
Calendar Service: Google Calendar Works best with Gmail.
Calendar App: Google Calendar This is what I am using now, but Google works fine with the built in app and great third party apps like Fantastical.
